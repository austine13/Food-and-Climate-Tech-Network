Sustainable Farming

This project focuses on sustainable farming practices to enhance food security. The objectives are to improve crop yields, promote eco-friendly techniques, and support local farmers. The expected outcomes include increased productivity and income for participating farmers.

Sustainable farming, as outlined in the Food and Climate Tech Network’s project portfolio, is presented as a cornerstone for enhancing food security through eco friendly practices. The initiative emphasizes improving crop yields while embedding techniques that protect ecosystems and support local farmers, ensuring that productivity gains do not come at the expense of long term soil and environmental health. By promoting climate smart methods such as regenerative soil management, the project aims to increase both productivity and farmer incomes. Beyond technical outcomes, the program actively engages communities by inviting volunteers to support training and outreach, and partners to validate agri tech innovations. This holistic approach positions sustainable farming not just as a set of practices, but as a movement that empowers youth and women, and scales climate smart agriculture.

Digital Advisory Platform

This digital platform provides farmers with real-time weather updates and market prices. Its objectives are to enhance decision-making and reduce losses due to climate variability, resulting in improved access to information and better market engagement.

Our digital advisory platforms connect farmers and stakeholders to real-time insights, agronomic advice, and market intelligence. Leveraging mobile apps, SMS services, and AI-driven dashboards, these platforms empower users to make informed decisions on crop planning, pest control, and resource allocation.

Aquaculture Development

This project promotes sustainable fish farming practices to enhance nutrition and livelihoods. It trains farmers in aquaculture techniques and aims to improve fish production, leading to increased fish yields and improved food security in target communities.

Climate Smart Training

This program equips farmers and extension officers with practical knowledge on climate-resilient agriculture. Through hands-on workshops, digital modules, and field demonstrations, participants learn how to adapt to changing weather patterns, improve soil health, resilience, and boost productivity using sustainable methods. The training fosters sustainable practices that enhance productivity while preserving ecosystems.

×

Innovation Test Bed

The Innovation Test Bed serves as a testing environment for piloting emerging technologies in agriculture. From IoT sensors to regenerative farming prototypes, this program enables experimentation in controlled environments. It supports startups, researchers, and communities to validate solutions, gather feedback, and scale innovations that address real-world challenges in food systems.
