Sustainable Farming

Description: This project focuses on sustainable farming practices to enhance food security.

Objectives: Improve crop yields, promote eco-friendly techniques, and support local farmers.

Outcomes: Increased productivity and income for participating farmers.

Digital Advisory Platform

Description: A digital platform providing farmers with real-time weather updates and market prices.

Objectives: Enhance decision-making and reduce losses due to climate variability.

Outcomes: Improved access to information and better market engagement.

Youth Training

Description: Training programs for youth in innovative agricultural techniques.

Objectives: Equip young people with skills for sustainable agriculture and entrepreneurship.

Outcomes: Increased youth participation in agriculture and job creation.

Aquaculture Development

Description: Promoting sustainable fish farming practices to enhance nutrition and livelihoods.

Objectives: Train farmers in aquaculture techniques and improve fish production.

Outcomes: Increased fish yields and improved food security in target communities.
