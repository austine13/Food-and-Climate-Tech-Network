About Food and Climate Tech Network

Who We Are

We are a leading organization dedicated to transforming agriculture through technology, innovation, and inclusive community engagement. We focus on empowering smallholder farmers, youth, and women to build resilient livelihoods and sustainable food systems.

We are a non-profit arm of Kilimo Hakika Limited, dedicated to driving inclusive livelihood transformation in Africa through pathways that build on local knowledge and community interest. We connect innovation with real-world impact by facilitating access to technologies, knowledge, and market systems for vulnerable and underserved farming communities. Our work is rooted in field-based evidence, inclusivity, gender equity, and sustainability.

Our Mission

To empower smallholder farmers, youth, and women through innovative solutions that promote sustainable agriculture and inclusive growth.

Our Vision

A world where every community has access to the resources and knowledge needed for resilient livelihoods.
