Our Programs

Climate Smart Training

This program equips farmers and extension officers with practical knowledge on climate-resilient agriculture. Through hands-on workshops, digital modules, and field demonstrations, participants learn how to adapt to changing weather patterns, improve soil health, and reduce carbon footprints. The training fosters sustainable practices that enhance productivity while preserving ecosystems.

Digital Advisory Platforms

Our digital advisory platforms connect farmers and stakeholders to real-time insights, agronomic advice, and market intelligence. Leveraging mobile apps, SMS services, and AI-driven dashboards, these platforms empower users to make informed decisions on crop planning, pest control, and resource allocation. The goal is to democratize access to expert guidance and bridge the digital divide in agriculture.

Innovation Test Bed

The Innovation Test Bed serves as a sandbox for piloting emerging technologies in agriculture. From IoT sensors to regenerative farming prototypes, this program enables experimentation in controlled environments. It supports startups, researchers, and communities to validate solutions, gather feedback, and scale innovations that address real-world challenges in food systems.
